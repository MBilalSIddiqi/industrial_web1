Ironclad began as a specialized metalworks facility in the heart of Germany's industrial sector. Our mission was simple: provide the most durable components for the world's most demanding machines.

Today, we have evolved into a global conglomerate, yet our core values remain unchanged. We believe in the honesty of materials, the precision of mathematics, and the power of hard work.

We don't build for the moment; we build for the next century.
